Burger recipe

  1. Take the ground beef
  2. Turn it into patty
  3. Grill it on 325 till even cooked on both sides
  4. Get your buns and place your lettuce on them
  5. Place the patty on the lettuce
  6. Then place the cheese on top of the patty
  7. Then place the tomato on the cheese
  8. Then the pickles
  9. Then use ketchep
  10. Then place the top bun to finish it off
